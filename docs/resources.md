### Glossary

**Readable run** means the work leaves a compact record: request, constraints, tool path, artifact, checks, uncertainty, and decision.

**Codex Model Orchestrator** is the control layer for readable AI-assisted work.

**MCP** is the tool contract layer: what the tool can read, what it can change, and what needs confirmation.

**App SDK surface** is the reader-facing view: status, action, result, evidence, and decision.

**Proof card** is a short written result that lets another person understand what happened.

**QA gate** is a check that turns a run into a decision: pass, revise, fail, blocked, or needs more review.

**Mob'ia / ccomf-unity** is the product layer around ComfyUI profiles, jobs, artifacts, and clients.

**LocalAssetFactory** is the local asset loop for generation, manifest, normalization, Unity import, and review.

### For Users, Testers, And Collaborators

Start with one workflow and one expected output. A useful review says what the person wanted, what the system did, what artifact exists, what was checked, what is unclear, and what the next decision should be.
