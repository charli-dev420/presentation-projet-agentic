<h2 id="english">🇬🇧 English</h2>

uwdevst_hub is the LLM-assisted synth and preset hub.

It can also appear in a music showcase because it concerns presets and sound candidates. In this repository, the focus is how the LLM helps generate, classify, compare, audition and evaluate musical material.

---

## LLM role

The LLM can help:

- generate preset directions;
- classify sounds by role or character;
- compare candidates;
- prepare listening notes;
- support evaluation and selection;
- summarize what should be kept, revised or rejected.

---

## Useful workflow

```text
preset direction → generation → audition → comparison → evaluation → selection note
```

The goal is to make sound exploration easier to review instead of treating presets as isolated outputs.

```text
direction preset → génération → écoute → comparaison → évaluation → note de sélection
```

Le but est de rendre l’exploration sonore plus facile à revoir au lieu de traiter les presets comme des sorties isolées.
