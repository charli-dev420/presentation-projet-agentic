# Partnership Brief / Fiche partenariat

## Francais

### Besoin

L'axe agentic cherche a transformer des agents outilles en produits controlables: orchestration, interfaces, validation, traces, permissions et collaboration humain-agent.

### Apports recherches

- Partenaires produit agentic.
- Retours sur Apps SDK, MCP et outils locaux.
- Expertise securite et gouvernance.
- Financement pour UX, validation, packaging et documentation.
- Opportunites emploi ou mission sur agents, orchestration et outils developpeur.

### Ce qui est partage publiquement

Une presentation et des principes. Les outils, endpoints, prompts, traces et implementations sensibles restent prives.

## English

### Need

The agentic track aims to turn tool-using agents into controllable products: orchestration, interfaces, validation, traces, permissions, and human-agent collaboration.

### Looking for

- Agentic product partners.
- Feedback on Apps SDK, MCP, and local tools.
- Security and governance expertise.
- Funding for UX, validation, packaging, and documentation.
- Employment or freelance opportunities around agents, orchestration, and developer tools.

### What is shared publicly

A presentation and principles. Tools, endpoints, prompts, traces, and sensitive implementations stay private.
