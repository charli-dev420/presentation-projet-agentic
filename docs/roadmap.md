# Roadmap / Feuille de route

[EN](#english) | [FR](#francais)

## English

### Product Direction

The roadmap starts with **Codex Model Orchestrator**. The priority is to make AI-assisted runs easier to scope, inspect, check, summarize, and hand back to a human.

CodexToUnity, Mob'ia / ccomf-unity, and LocalAssetFactory develop in parallel as practical proof surfaces: Unity handoff, ComfyUI product jobs, and local asset validation.

### Near Term

Keep the project dossier readable: README, overview, project pages, user flows, resources, evidence, and QA pages should tell the same story.

Prepare one strong readable-run scenario and one Unity/ComfyUI asset scenario.

Clarify result-card language: request, constraints, artifact, checks, uncertainty, decision, next action.

### Mid Term

Improve MCP/App SDK state views around status, confirmations, proof cards, and decision surfaces.

Strengthen CodexToUnity handoff criteria and LocalAssetFactory asset review criteria.

Clarify Mob'ia / ccomf-unity job lifecycle wording for users and collaborators.

### Longer Term

Use the documentation and validation structure to support technical collaboration, funding, mission work, hiring, and product partnerships. The goal is to make each next workflow understandable and testable.

## Francais

### Direction Produit

La roadmap commence par **Codex Model Orchestrator**. La priorite est de rendre les runs assistes par IA plus faciles a cadrer, inspecter, controler, resumer et remettre a un humain.

CodexToUnity, Mob'ia / ccomf-unity et LocalAssetFactory avancent en parallele comme surfaces de preuve pratiques: handoff Unity, jobs produit ComfyUI et validation asset locale.

### Court Terme

Garder le dossier projet lisible: README, overview, pages projet, user flows, resources, evidence et QA doivent raconter la meme histoire.

Preparer un scenario fort de run lisible et un scenario asset Unity/ComfyUI.

Clarifier le langage carte resultat: demande, contraintes, artefact, controles, incertitude, decision, prochaine action.

### Moyen Terme

Ameliorer les vues etat MCP/App SDK autour de statut, confirmations, proof cards et surfaces de decision.

Renforcer les criteres handoff CodexToUnity et les criteres revue asset LocalAssetFactory.

Clarifier le wording cycle de vie job Mob'ia / ccomf-unity pour utilisateurs et collaborateurs.

### Plus Long Terme

Utiliser la documentation et la structure de validation pour soutenir collaboration technique, financement, mission, recrutement et partenariats produit. Le but est de rendre chaque prochain workflow comprehensible et testable.
