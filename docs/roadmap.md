# Roadmap / Feuille de route

[EN](#english) | [FR](#francais)

## English

The roadmap is organized around proof-led productization: make the workflow easier to understand, easier to validate, and easier to discuss with partners without weakening the public/private boundary.

| Horizon | Goal | Public output |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Short term | Complete the showcase documentation. | Stronger README, one-pager, project pages, scenarios, proof pack, QA matrix. |
| Short term | Make proof levels easy to use. | Clear L0-L5 claim language and proof-card template. |
| Short term | Improve buyer and partner paths. | Buyer evaluation, partnership brief, decision pack, contact reasons. |
| Mid term | Stabilize bounded demos. | Orchestrator proof loop, Apps SDK/MCP surface review, Unity bridge demo, ComfyUI product job. |
| Mid term | Strengthen UX and safety framing. | Read/write separation, human confirmation, redaction rules, dashboard/card model. |
| Mid term | Create private proof packs. | Redacted summaries from live or dry-run scenarios, with QA and human decision. |
| Long term | Move toward partner pilots. | Scoped pilot plan, evaluation criteria, security review, reproducible proof language. |

### Product Direction

The next useful work is not adding more names. It is taking one or two concrete workflows and showing the full path from user goal to controlled run, QA result, proof card, and human decision.

## Francais

La roadmap est organisee autour d'une productisation orientee preuve: rendre le workflow plus comprehensible, plus validable et plus facile a discuter avec partenaires sans affaiblir la frontiere public/prive.

| Horizon | Objectif | Sortie publique |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Court terme | Completer la documentation vitrine. | README, one-pager, pages projet, scenarios, proof pack et matrice QA renforces. |
| Court terme | Rendre les niveaux de preuve faciles a utiliser. | Langage claims L0-L5 clair et template de proof card. |
| Court terme | Ameliorer les parcours acheteur et partenaire. | Buyer evaluation, partnership brief, decision pack, raisons de contact. |
| Moyen terme | Stabiliser des demos bornees. | Proof loop orchestrateur, revue surface Apps SDK/MCP, demo pont Unity, job produit ComfyUI. |
| Moyen terme | Renforcer cadrage UX et securite. | Separation read/write, confirmation humaine, regles redaction, modele dashboard/carte. |
| Moyen terme | Creer des proof packs prives. | Resumes rediges de scenarios live ou dry-run, avec QA et decision humaine. |
| Long terme | Aller vers des pilotes partenaires. | Plan pilote cadre, criteres evaluation, revue securite, langage preuve reproductible. |

### Direction Produit

Le prochain travail utile n'est pas d'ajouter plus de noms. C'est de prendre un ou deux workflows concrets et montrer le chemin complet de l'objectif utilisateur au run controle, resultat QA, proof card et decision humaine.
