# Evidence Ledger / Registre des preuves

[EN](#english) | [FR](#francais)

## English

This ledger keeps claims tied to proof level and public artifact. It is designed for readers who need to know what can be trusted now, what needs a private demo, and what should not be overclaimed.

| ID | Surface | Level | Public status | Allowed claim | Public artifact |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| `AG-EV-01` | Codex Model Orchestrator | L1 | Published method | Agentic outputs can be qualified with proof levels, QA gates, and human decisions. | [Proof pack](proof-pack.md), [QA matrix](qa-matrix.md) |
| `AG-EV-02` | Codex Model Orchestrator | L1-L2 | Measurement model | Value can be discussed through accepted-task cost, failure rate, human score, and correction time. | [Evidence](evidence.md), [buyer evaluation](buyer-evaluation.md) |
| `AG-EV-03` | Apps SDK / MCP | L1 | Product-surface model | Readable status, controlled actions, and decision cards are the right public shape for agentic work. | [Tutorials](tutorials.md), [ecosystem map](ecosystem-map.md) |
| `AG-EV-04` | Human workflow | L1 | User-flow documentation | A result is not complete until a human can accept, revise, reject, or escalate it. | [User flows](user-flows.md), [decision pack](decision-pack.md) |
| `AG-EV-05` | CodexToUnity | L1-L2 | Public prototype framing | An agentic workflow can be scoped toward a verifiable Unity handoff. | [CodexToUnity](projects/codextounity.md), [Unity scenario](scenarios/unity-asset-loop.md) |
| `AG-EV-06` | Mob'ia / ccomf-unity | L1 | Product map | ComfyUI workflows can be wrapped in profiles, jobs, artifacts, client status, and review decisions. | [Mob'ia / ccomf-unity](projects/mobia-ccomf-unity.md), [ComfyUI scenario](scenarios/comfyui-product-layer.md) |
| `AG-EV-07` | LocalAssetFactory | L1 | Workflow diagram | The asset loop can be explained as intent, job, manifest, normalization, Unity import, and review. | [Visual gallery](visual-gallery.md), [LocalAssetFactory](projects/local-asset-factory.md) |
| `AG-EV-08` | Public governance | L1 | Redaction policy | Public material can stay useful without leaking prompts, traces, endpoints, tokens, private assets, or workflow internals. | [Public scope](public-scope.md), [redaction policy](redaction-policy.md) |

### Reading Rule

Use the lowest supported level when discussing a claim. A public L1 method can be valuable and credible, but it should not be sold as L4 independent reproduction until that evidence exists.

Ce registre relie les claims a un niveau de preuve et un artefact public. Il sert aux lecteurs qui doivent savoir ce qui est fiable maintenant, ce qui demande une demo privee et ce qui ne doit pas etre survendu.

| ID | Surface | Niveau | Statut public | Claim autorise | Artefact public |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| `AG-EV-01` | Codex Model Orchestrator | L1 | Methode publiee | Les sorties agentiques peuvent etre qualifiees par niveaux de preuve, gates QA et decisions humaines. | [Proof pack](proof-pack.md), [QA matrix](qa-matrix.md) |
| `AG-EV-02` | Codex Model Orchestrator | L1-L2 | Modele de mesure | La valeur se discute par cout par tache acceptee, taux d'echec, score humain et temps de correction. | [Evidence](evidence.md), [buyer evaluation](buyer-evaluation.md) |
| `AG-EV-03` | Apps SDK / MCP | L1 | Modele de surface produit | Statut lisible, actions controlees et cartes de decision sont la bonne forme publique du travail agentique. | [Tutorials](tutorials.md), [ecosystem map](ecosystem-map.md) |
| `AG-EV-04` | Workflow humain | L1 | Documentation de flux | Un resultat n'est complet que si un humain peut accepter, reviser, refuser ou escalader. | [User flows](user-flows.md), [decision pack](decision-pack.md) |
| `AG-EV-05` | CodexToUnity | L1-L2 | Cadrage prototype public | Un workflow agentique peut etre cadre vers un handoff Unity verifiable. | [CodexToUnity](projects/codextounity.md), [scenario Unity](scenarios/unity-asset-loop.md) |
| `AG-EV-06` | Mob'ia / ccomf-unity | L1 | Carte produit | Des workflows ComfyUI peuvent etre enveloppes par profils, jobs, artefacts, statuts clients et decisions de revue. | [Mob'ia / ccomf-unity](projects/mobia-ccomf-unity.md), [scenario ComfyUI](scenarios/comfyui-product-layer.md) |

### Regle De Lecture

Utiliser le niveau supporte le plus bas quand on discute un claim. Une methode publique L1 peut etre utile et credible, mais ne doit pas etre vendue comme reproduction independante L4 tant que cette preuve n'existe pas.
