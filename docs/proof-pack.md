# Proof Pack / Pack de preuves

[EN](#english) | [FR](#francais)

## English

### Purpose

The proof pack turns a run into a small product record. A reader should understand what was attempted, what artifact exists, how it was checked, what remains uncertain, and what a person decided.

### Result Card Shape

- Task class: Orchestrator run, Unity handoff, ComfyUI product job, local asset review, or MCP/App SDK surface.
- Scope: goal, allowed actions, expected output, and constraints.
- Artifact: file, state, manifest, card, diagram, client status, or written result.
- Checks: QA criteria used for the surface.
- Evidence: what supports the claim.
- Decision: accept, revise, reject, archive, escalate, or keep reviewing.
- Next action: the smallest useful follow-up.

### How It Helps

The proof pack keeps the project practical. It prevents a run from being judged only by how confident the text sounds. The important question is whether another person can read the record and understand the decision.

## Francais

### Objectif

Le proof pack transforme un run en petite fiche produit. Un lecteur doit comprendre ce qui a ete tente, quel artefact existe, comment il a ete controle, ce qui reste incertain et quelle decision humaine a suivi.

### Forme De Carte Resultat

- Classe de tache: run orchestrateur, handoff Unity, job produit ComfyUI, revue asset locale ou surface MCP/App SDK.
- Perimetre: objectif, actions autorisees, sortie attendue et contraintes.
- Artefact: fichier, etat, manifest, carte, diagramme, statut client ou resultat ecrit.
- Controles: criteres QA utilises pour la surface.
- Preuve: ce qui soutient le claim.
- Decision: accepter, reviser, refuser, archiver, escalader ou continuer la revue.
- Prochaine action: le plus petit suivi utile.

### Pourquoi C'Est Utile

Le proof pack garde le projet pratique. Il evite de juger un run seulement au niveau de confiance du texte. La question importante est de savoir si une autre personne peut lire la fiche et comprendre la decision.
